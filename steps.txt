1. open web page https://www.saucedemo.com/inventory.html
2. enter login and password (login = standard_user, password = secret_sauce) and press "Login" button
3. choose last two items on the page and press "add to cart" buttons
4. move to cart and check the presence of added items
5. delete one of the items by pressing the remove button.
6. check the cart for absence of the removed item
7. press "checkout" and make sure that you have been moved to your information page
8. enter first name, last name, zip/postal code and press "continue" button
9. make sure that we are on the order processing page
10. press "finish" button. make sure that we are on finished order page
11. click on "back home" button. check that we are on product page again
